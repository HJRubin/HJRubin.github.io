Section 2: My research.

I'm currently working on a NASA IDS project to remotely sense lake water clarity with Landsat and a Random Forest algorithm. But I suppose my dissertation will probably be on measurement model fusion for nitrogen deposition. That means combining in situ measurements with chemical transport models and climate data and remotely sensed deposition estimates to create better predictions and maps. To be honest I don't really care about nitrogen but I really care about maps.
