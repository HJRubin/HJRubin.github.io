About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, specializing in future climate impacts with a minor in computation science. My dissertation focuses on mapping techniques with earth system model output, from using a new measurement-model fusion method to update the global nitrogen deposition budget, to mapping patterns of sulfur deposition under a future climate scenario with sulfate aerosol injection geoengineering.
